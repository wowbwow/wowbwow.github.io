I've registered an account, how can I view the content related to my child?

You need to follow your child's account.
PC: Activity feed →In "My Family," click on "Follow Student Account." →Enter the student account of your child. →Follow
Mobile phone, tablet: My →Click on "Follow Student Account". →Click on "Add Follow". →Enter the student account of your child. →Follow
After following, you can see content related to your child in the activity feed.

What is a parent account?

The wowbwow account registered using your email is referred to as the "parent account".

If you entered the wrong email during registration or used the wrong email, can it be corrected/modified?

Yes, you can. Please send an email or contact us to inform the email address you intend to use as your account and the password. Our customer service representatives will assist you in changing it to the correct account. You can update the password yourself after logging in.

I have accumulated rewards in my account, how can I ensure the security of my account?

Please update accurate information in your personal profile, such as phone number, email, etc. The completeness and accuracy of your information will help ensure the security of your account.

What is a student account? How can I obtain a student account?

"A student account" is an account provided by the school or class for the child to use. Children cannot register their accounts like parents do.
If you need a student account, please contact the school or class where your child is enrolled.

Can a parent account only follow one child?

The system doesn't have a limit on the number of accounts that can be followed; parents can follow multiple children.

I'm trying to install wowbwow on my iPad (Apple tablet), but I can't find the app?

Please choose to search for the app designed for iPhone, and you will be able to find "wowbwow". Although the "wowbwow" app is categorized under iPhone, it can also be installed and used on an iPad.

Why am I not receiving push notifications sent by the teachers?

The iOS system (Apple) will prompt a message the first time you use it, asking if you allow (grant access to) push notifications. If you choose not to allow, you won't receive any push notifications from wowbwow.
Enable push notification settings: In the app, click on "My" → slide the "Push Notification Settings" to the right to turn it green, then follow the system's prompts to enable the settings.

Assisting my child with recording for learning, pressed the microphone button but couldn't capture any sound?

The iOS system (Apple) will prompt a message the first time you use it, asking if you allow (grant access to) microphone usage. If you choose not to allow, even if you press the microphone button within the app, recording won't work. In such cases, please go to the device's "Settings" and set the microphone to the "Allow" status.

Why haven't I been receiving messages from the school?

Please contact the teacher to initiate the use of wowbwow for school-parent communication.
