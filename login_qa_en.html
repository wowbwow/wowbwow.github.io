How to register wowbwow account?

Use your email address to register for a wowbwow account.

Is it necessary to register using an email address?

Yes, using the correct email address can ensure the security of your account. When you lose your password, you can reset it through the email address.

What is a parent account?

The wowbwow account registered with your email address is considered a "Parent Account."

Can I log in with my child's account?

The "Wowbwow" app is exclusively for parents, please log in using your parent account.

Is it possible to register a wowbwow account using the child's account?

No, it's not possible. Each account in the system is unique. The child's account is considered a "Student Account" in the wowbwow system and cannot be used to register as a "Parent Account." Please use your email address to register for your dedicated wowbwow parent account.

Can teachers log in to the "Wowbwow" app?

The "Wowbwow" app is exclusively for parents, and teacher accounts cannot log in. Please use the dedicated apps for teachers, such as "Attend EZ" and "Teacher EZ."

What should I do if I forget my password?

You can use the system's "Forgot Password" feature by entering the email address used for registration or the email address provided in your personal information. The system will send an email to that address allowing you to reset your password.

What should I do if I forget my account?

If your account's personal information is filled out completely, please email us or call us to provide details such as the "username" and "phone number." We can assist you in retrieving your account.

Why can't I log in even though I've entered the correct account and password?

Please first confirm if you are using a parent account.
When entering your account and password, please pay attention to:
1. Please ensure there are no extra spaces before the account.
2. For passwords, please note that English letters are case-sensitive, meaning uppercase and lowercase letters are treated as distinct. Also, ensure there are no extra spaces. In case of multiple login failures, the system temporarily restricts access for account security. You can try again after half an hour.

If I entered the wrong email address during registration or used the incorrect email address, can I make changes?

Certainly. Please send an email or call us to inform the email address you intend to use as your account and the password. Our customer service staff will assist you in changing it to the correct account. You can modify the password yourself after logging in.
